Order-to-Cash Optimization (Microsoft Dynamics NAV18)

Distribution client · via JL13 Concepts
Problem

Order-to-Cash was breaking across order entry, inventory allocation, and shipment processing, with fulfilment stuck at 78% on-time and recurring inventory variances.

Approach

Interviewed 14 stakeholders to map the current-state workflow, pulled and reconciled six months of NAV18 transaction data to find root causes, and identified three systemic gaps: missing shipment notification triggers, a manual CRM-to-NAV re-entry loop, and UOM mismatches at receiving. Delivered six targeted configuration changes, including a receiving validation rule and corrected UOM conversion tables.

35% faster order cycle time · on-time fulfilment up from 78% to 94% · stock variance down 25%

AI Support Chatbot: Requirements & Conversation Design

Fashion e-commerce platform, 15,000+ monthly visitors · via Heels & Tech
Problem

The support team was manually handling every customer query on a fast-growing e-commerce site, with no visibility into which query types were actually driving ticket volume.

Approach

Analyzed three months of support ticket logs to identify the 12 query types driving 70% of volume, documented the conversational flows as a decision tree, and defined chatbot scope and handoff rules, signed off by the product owner before development started.

Consulting Exercise

Business Systems Analyst Consulting Exercise: Triage, Incident Response & Program Redesign

Consulting case exercise for a nonprofit organization · organization name withheld
Brief

A prospective client organization presented three realistic operational scenarios, competing priorities with no formal triage process, a 3-day CRM integration failure, and a fragmented monthly-giving payments program, and asked for a live, defended consulting approach with no scripted answer.

Approach

Applied the same method across all three: triage by impact rather than urgency, run parallel tracks to unblock the business while root-causing in parallel, elicit across all stakeholder teams before designing, quantify the problem before proposing a fix, and require a signed-off RACI before anything gets built.

Modeled $70K+ in estimated annual unrecovered revenue as a testable hypothesis · full RACI, dunning sequence, and 5-6 month phased rollout plan presented and defended live
